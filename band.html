Twenty One Pilots


Twenty One Pilots (stylized in all lowercase or as twenty øne piløts) is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. It consists of Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun.


The band is famous for their unique sound and quirky lyrics. Twenty one pilots combine musical styles, vocals and instrumentals that might seem like they won't go together at first glance, but the resulting songs are a bliss. This band has always been true to themselves and their art, so their music is always raw, emotional and really inspiring.


Twenty one pilots do not just create music - they are artists that have created an entire world full of complex stories and distinct characters. The band loves creating games, visuals, riddles and various other surprises for their fans, and the fans show their love by fully immersing themselves in the world of twenty one pilots, creating unique art, amazing looks for the concerts, surprises for the artists and more.


Their latest project, implemented during the quarantine, was a riddle for fans where they had to search for codes all over the internet to unlock rare content. The final prize was the access to their newest fresh idea - never-ending music video of their song "Level Of Concern" (LOC) which fans can parttake in by submitting their own photos or short videos.
